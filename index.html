Welcome to Cosmic Fox Game Studios!

We're a small indie team from Brazil, passionate about creating narrative-driven games.

Cosmic Fox began at a Game Jam, where our team connected and decided to expand our initial project into something bigger.

Our goal is to create stories that celebrate diversity, bringing fresh perspectives to the gaming world while also delivering fun and immersive experiences.
